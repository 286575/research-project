Results

Results are crucial for a soccer player because they reflect the success of their individual and team efforts, motivating them to perform better and achieve their goals. Positive results can lead to recognition, opportunities for advancement, and the chance to compete at higher levels. Additionally, they build confidence and demonstrate the value of hard work, discipline, and teamwork on the field.
